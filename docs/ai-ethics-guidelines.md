# AI Ethics Guidelines

## Review Checklist for AI Model Release

Before a new or materially updated model is released to production, the
following review checklist must be completed and signed off by the AI
Governance Committee:

- [ ] Intended-use statement published
- [ ] Training-data provenance documented
- [ ] Bias evaluation completed across protected attributes
- [ ] Adversarial-input test cases passed
- [ ] Rollback plan defined
- [ ] Monitoring and drift-detection configured
- [ ] Customer-facing disclosures updated
- [ ] Final report filed in the model registry

## Stakeholder Involvement

Every AI initiative with material customer impact requires documented
involvement of: product management, engineering, legal/privacy, and
at least one external domain expert for high-risk use cases.

## Incident Handling

Suspected bias incidents, unexpected model behaviour, or privacy
concerns are reported via the standard incident-response channel and
additionally to the AI Governance Committee within 24 hours.
